About International Turon Federation

Preserving tradition, building the future

Our Mission

The International Turon Federation (ITF) serves as the global governing body for traditional Turon sports, dedicated to preserving these ancient athletic traditions while promoting their growth and development worldwide.

We believe that traditional sports are not just competitions, but cultural bridges that connect nations, preserve heritage, and inspire future generations to embrace their roots while reaching for excellence.

Our Vision

To establish Turon sports as recognized and celebrated disciplines on the global stage, fostering international cooperation and cultural exchange through athletic excellence.

We envision a world where traditional sports thrive alongside modern athletics, creating opportunities for athletes from all backgrounds to compete, learn, and grow together.

Our Values

  • Heritage Preservation: Maintaining the authenticity and cultural significance of traditional Turon sports
  • Global Unity: Bringing together nations through shared sporting experiences
  • Excellence: Promoting the highest standards in competition and sportsmanship
  • Innovation: Adapting traditional practices for modern global participation
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring equal opportunities for all athletes regardless of background
