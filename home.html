About International Turon Federation

The International Turon Federation (ITF) is the global governing body for traditional Turon sports, dedicated to preserving and promoting these ancient athletic traditions worldwide.

Founded with the mission to unite nations through sport, we organize international competitions, develop standardized rules, and support the growth of Turon sports in member countries.
