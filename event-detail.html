Event Overview

The World Turon Championship 2024 marks the 29th edition of our premier international competition, bringing together the finest athletes from across the globe to compete in traditional Turon sports disciplines.

This year's championship promises to be the most competitive yet, with record participation from 28 member countries and over 500 athletes competing across 8 traditional disciplines. The event will showcase the rich cultural heritage of Turon sports while celebrating athletic excellence at the highest level.

Event Highlights

  • Opening ceremony featuring traditional cultural performances
  • Competition across 8 traditional Turon disciplines
  • International coaching workshop and seminars
  • Cultural exchange programs and exhibitions
  • Awards ceremony with traditional honors
